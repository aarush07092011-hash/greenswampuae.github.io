🌍 Our Mission

About & Impact

A student on a mission to reduce waste, save money, and build community.

🌱

Hi, I'm the founder of GreenSwap UAE 👋

I'm a high-school freshman based in the UAE. Growing up here, I noticed how much perfectly good clothing and books ended up in the bin — especially at the end of each school year.

I started GreenSwap UAE because I believe we can do better. Every item that's reused is one less item in a landfill, and one more family who doesn't have to pay full price for new things.

What began as a small WhatsApp group has grown into pop-up events, an online shop, and a real community of families who care about the planet and their wallets.

This is just the beginning. 🌿
